I am a Ph.D. student in iQua at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering in the University of Toronto,advised by Baochun Li. I received my bachelor degree from Department of CSE in Shanghai Jiao Tong University. During my undergraduate studies, I was a research intern at John Hopcroft Center for Computer Science, advised by Liyao Xiang.Previously, I took a research fellowship at Max Planck Society, advised by Yiting Xia and Binghang Yuan. My research interests are in Trustworthy ML and learning in distributed systems, in applications of federated learning and split learning. I’m interested in solving problems with real-world use cases, taking into account privacy, scalability and efficiency.
